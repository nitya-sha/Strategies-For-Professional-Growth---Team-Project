BSGN3001: Strategies for Professional Growth (SPG)

Personal Productivity as a Key Skill for the Future of Work

---

## 📌 Project Overview

This project was created as part of the **BSGN3001: Strategies for Professional Growth (SPG)** course (January 2026). Our research focuses on how **personal productivity is emerging as a critical skill in the evolving future of work**, shaped by digitalisation, AI, hybrid work models, and rising demands for self-management.

The project covers:
- The distinction between being busy and being genuinely productive
- Insights from interviews with **5 working professionals** across Healthcare IT, Property Insurance, IT Consulting, Education, and Software Engineering
- Analysis of secondary resources (HBR, MIT Sloan, TED Talks, books by Drucker, Newport, and Covey)
- Alignment with the **WEF Future of Jobs 2025** report and its Core Skills 2030
- Strategic recommendations for students and future professionals
